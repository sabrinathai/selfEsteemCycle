Daily Experiences Study App Download Page
iPhone/iPad version 1.0.0
Install iOS version 1.0.0

Follow the instructions on the page. If you need help with installing the app, click Help on the sidebar.



After downloading the app, go to Settings > General.
Scroll down to VPN & Device Management, and then click on it.
You will see Embodied Social Cognition under Enterprise App.
Click on Embodied Social Cognition and then click on Trust "Embodied Social Cognition".



Android version 1.0.0
Install Android version 1.0.0

1. After downloading the app, go to Settings > Security.
2. Check the option "Unknown sources".
3. Tap OK on the prompt message.
4. Select "Trust".
